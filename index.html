Game modes

Four ways to make chat play

Questions

Good to know

Is it really free?＋
Yes. The free version includes every game mode and feature, with a 1 hour/day limit on live-connected play and one platform at a time. Pro is a one-time purchase that removes the limit and unlocks both platforms at once.
Do I need anything to connect to TikTok?＋
TikTok works right away through a free shared connection. For the most reliable results, a built-in setup screen helps you grab your own free key (about a minute) so you're on your own quota. Twitch needs nothing extra.
Will Windows warn me when I open it?＋
It may show a SmartScreen notice because the app isn't code-signed yet — click “More info” then “Run anyway.” It runs entirely on your own computer.
Does it work with OBS / Streamlabs?＋
Yes — the overlay is a standard browser source at 1920×1080, so it works in OBS, Streamlabs and anything that supports browser sources.
Is my account safe?＋
The app connects to public live chat the same way a viewer does. It never asks for your TikTok or Twitch password, and everything runs locally on your machine.
